What Is Council Tax?

Council tax is a local tax paid by households in the UK. It helps pay for services such as rubbish collection, police, fire services, and street lighting.

How council tax bands work

Homes are placed into bands (A–H in England) based on their value in 1991. Each band pays a different amount.

Who has to pay council tax?

Usually the person living in the property. If you live alone, you may qualify for a 25% discount.

Ways to reduce your bill

  • Single person discount
  • Student exemption
  • Disability reduction
  • Challenging your band in certain cases
